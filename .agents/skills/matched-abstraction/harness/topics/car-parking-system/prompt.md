# Topic Prompt: Car Parking System

Design a medium-size car parking management system for an operator that runs several multi-level parking facilities in one city.

The system should support drivers, lot attendants, facility managers, and operations staff. It should cover vehicle entry and exit, spot availability, ticketing or session tracking, payments, reservations or permits, manual overrides, facility configuration, and operational reporting.

Important conditions:

- Facilities may have gated entrances and exits, license plate cameras, payment kiosks, and attendant consoles.
- Availability must be accurate enough for drivers and operators, but hardware events can be delayed or wrong.
- Pricing and permit rules can vary by facility, time, vehicle type, and special events.
- The operator needs a plan that can later produce implementation tasks, test work, integration work, and rollout work.

Use matched abstraction and stop at Plan.
